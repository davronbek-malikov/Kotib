Maxfiylik siyosati

Ilova: Kotib — aqlli shaxsiy kotib · Kuchga kirgan sana: 2026-yil 17-iyul

1. Qisqacha

Kotib hisob (akkaunt) talab qilmaydi. Rejalaringiz faqat sizning telefoningizda saqlanadi. Bizda foydalanuvchi ma'lumotlari bazasi yo'q. Yagona istisno — «AI yordamchi»: undan savol so'rasangiz, savolingiz va rejalaringiz sun'iy intellekt xizmatiga yuboriladi (4-bo'limga qarang).

2. Qanday ma'lumot saqlanadi va qayerda

Siz kiritgan hamma narsa — vazifalar, sanalar, vaqtlar, ro'yxatlar va sozlamalar — qurilmangiz xotirasida (localStorage va Android ilovasida qurilma xotirasi) saqlanadi. Bu ma'lumotlar bizning serverimizga yuborilmaydi va u yerda saqlanmaydi.

Biz ism, telefon raqami, elektron pochta yoki joylashuv kabi shaxsni aniqlovchi ma'lumotlarni so'ramaymiz va yig'maymiz.

3. Hisob va ro'yxatdan o'tish

Yo'q. Kotibdan foydalanish uchun ro'yxatdan o'tish shart emas.

4. AI yordamchi — muhim

«AI yordamchi»ga savol yozganingizda, quyidagilar HTTPS orqali bizning serverimizdagi vositachi funksiyaga, u orqali esa Groq (groq.com) sun'iy intellekt xizmatiga yuboriladi:

  • savolingiz matni;
  • oxirgi 30 kun va keyingi 90 kun ichidagi vazifalaringiz — nomi, sanasi, vaqti, toifasi, bajarilgani;
  • ro'yxatlaringiz nomi va ulardagi elementlar;
  • sozlamalaringiz (til, uslub, rejim) va bugungi sana;
  • shu suhbatdagi oxirgi bir necha xabar.

Bu javob berish uchun kerak: yordamchi «ertaga nima bor?» degan savolga faqat rejalaringizni ko'rgandagina javob bera oladi.

Biz bu ma'lumotni saqlamaymiz. Vositachi funksiya javobni sizga uzatadi va hech narsani yozib qo'ymaydi — bizda log yoki baza yo'q. Groq tomonidagi qayta ishlash ularning maxfiylik siyosatiga bo'ysunadi.

Nazorat sizda: AI yordamchidan foydalanmasangiz, hech narsa yuborilmaydi. Ilovaning qolgan qismi (rejalar, taqvim, ro'yxatlar, eslatmalar) internetsiz ham, hech narsa yubormasdan ham ishlayveradi.

5. Eslatmalar

Eslatmalar telefoningizning o'zida ishlaydi. Ular bizning serverimizdan kelmaydi va push-xizmat ishlatilmaydi — shuning uchun eslatma uchun hech qanday ma'lumot yuborilmaydi.

6. Uchinchi tomon xizmatlari

  • Vercel — ilova joylashgan xosting. Har qanday veb-saytdagi kabi, server so'rovlar haqida texnik yozuvlar (IP manzil, vaqt) yuritishi mumkin.
  • Groq — faqat AI yordamchi uchun (4-bo'lim).
  • Google Fonts — shrift yuklanadi, bu Google'ga IP manzilingizni ko'rsatadi.

Biz reklama, analitika va kuzatuv (tracking) vositalarini ishlatmaymiz. Ma'lumotlaringizni sotmaymiz.

7. Ma'lumotni o'chirish

Sozlamalar → Ma'lumotlar → «Hammasini o'chirish» tugmasi hamma narsani o'chiradi. Ilovani o'chirib tashlasangiz ham ma'lumot qurilmangiz bilan birga ketadi. Bizda o'chiriladigan nusxa yo'q.

Sozlamalar → Ma'lumotlar → «Zaxira nusxa» orqali hamma ma'lumotingizni JSON fayl sifatida o'zingizga yuklab olishingiz mumkin.

8. Bolalar

Kotib umumiy foydalanuvchilar uchun. Biz bolalardan ataylab ma'lumot yig'maymiz — umuman hech kimdan yig'maymiz.

9. O'zgarishlar

Bu siyosat o'zgarsa, shu sahifadagi sana yangilanadi. Muhim o'zgarish bo'lsa, ilova ichida xabar beramiz.

Privacy Policy (English)

App: Kotib — smart personal secretary · Effective: 17 July 2026

1. Summary

Kotib requires no account. Your plans are stored only on your phone. We run no user database. The single exception is the AI assistant: when you ask it something, your question and your plans are sent to an AI service (see section 4).

2. What is stored, and where

Everything you enter — tasks, dates, times, lists and settings — is stored in your device's own storage (localStorage, plus native device storage in the Android app). It is not sent to us and not stored by us.

We do not ask for or collect identifying information such as your name, phone number, email or location.

3. Accounts

None. No sign-up is needed to use Kotib.

4. The AI assistant — important

When you write to the AI assistant, the following is sent over HTTPS to a proxy function on our server, and from there to Groq (groq.com):

  • your question;
  • your tasks from the last 30 and next 90 days — title, date, time, category, and whether they are done;
  • your checklists and their items;
  • your settings (language, style, mode) and today's date;
  • the last few messages of that conversation.

This is what makes an answer possible: the assistant can only answer "what's on tomorrow?" if it can see your plans.

We do not store any of it. The proxy passes the answer back to you and keeps nothing — we hold no logs and no database. Processing on Groq's side is governed by their privacy policy.

You are in control: if you don't use the AI assistant, nothing is ever sent. The rest of the app — plans, calendar, lists, reminders — works without internet and without sending anything.

5. Reminders

Reminders are scheduled by your phone itself. They do not come from our server and no push service is used, so nothing is transmitted for a reminder to fire.

6. Third parties

  • Vercel — hosting. As with any website, the server may keep technical request logs (IP address, timestamp).
  • Groq — for the AI assistant only (section 4).
  • Google Fonts — loading fonts exposes your IP address to Google.

We use no advertising, no analytics and no tracking. We do not sell your data.

7. Deleting your data

Settings → Data → "Delete everything" erases all of it. Uninstalling the app also removes it with the device. There is no copy on our side to delete.

Settings → Data → "Export backup" downloads all your data to yourself as a JSON file.

8. Children

Kotib is a general-audience app. We do not knowingly collect data from children — we do not collect data from anyone.

9. Changes

If this policy changes, the date on this page is updated. For significant changes we will say so inside the app.
